About Us

Welcome to Illuminate 2k23!

Illuminate is a technical fest that brings together the best minds to connect, create and celebrate creativity, knowledge and fun.

  • This fest will feature a wide range of exciting activities.
  • It provides a unique opportunity for the students to learn from each other, exchange ideas and collaborate.
  • There's something for everyone from tech talks to dance.

We have put together an amazing lineup of events and activities that will help you get a glimpse into the future of tech. From hackathons, tech talks to dance, music and more Illuminate 2k23! So join us to explore and unleash your creative powers. Don't miss your chance to CONNECT, CREATE, CELEBRATE with ILLUMINATE-2K23.
